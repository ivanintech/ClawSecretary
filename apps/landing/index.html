How It Works

From Zero to Autonomous in 3 Steps

01

Scan & Connect

Open the SaaS dashboard, scan the WhatsApp QR code, and click your Google/Outlook account. Zero-Touch OAuth handles the rest.

02

Magic Activation

Hit the "Magic Activation" button. The secretary registers its own autonomous crons — daily briefings and pre-meeting research — instantly.

03

Let It Work

Every morning at 08h you get your briefing on WhatsApp. Every :45 it checks for upcoming meetings and researches them. You just live your life.

🧠

Persistent Intelligence with WAL Protocol

Unlike chatbots that forget, ClawSecretary uses a Write-Ahead Log — every preference, decision, and action is persisted to SESSION-STATE.md before execution. It remembers like a real assistant.

  • ✅ Never loses context across sessions
  • ✅ Learns your preferences autonomously
  • ✅ Full audit trail of every decision
  • ✅ Memory freshening via weekly autonomous cron
